Everstem Education

A non-profit organization run by students for students

Everstem is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization based in Washington State, USA. We are dedicated to making STEM educational opportunities more accessible for all students through free workshops, seminars, hackathon events and providing grants and mentorship for new FIRST Lego League (FLL) and FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)
